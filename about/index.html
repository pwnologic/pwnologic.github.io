About pwnologic

Initiative focused on identifying vulnerabilities in emerging technologies and developing open-source defensive tools.

Founded in 2025, pwnologic brings together security researchers and developers with a shared commitment to improving digital security.

Our primary areas of focus:

  • IoT Security Testing - Creating standardized methodologies for assessing smart device security and publishing findings to improve the ecosystem.
  • Open-Source Monitoring - Building lightweight security tools that enable organizations of all sizes to detect and respond to threats.

We operate with a minimalist approach, focusing on efficiency and practical solutions rather than complexity.

pwnologic is committed to responsible disclosure and collaborates with manufacturers and developers to address vulnerabilities before public release.
